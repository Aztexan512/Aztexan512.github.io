Revenue Expansion

MPHH Cross-Sell Propensity & Revenue Expansion

Identifies which single-product AutoShield Insurance households are most likely to add a second product within 12 months. Gradient Boosting model (AUC 0.69) on 150,000 synthetic households across a 3-year window. Property quote abandonment and proactive outreach frequency are the two strongest behavioral signals. Top two deciles capture 36% of all conversions. Projected unconverted CLTV opportunity exceeds $280M.
